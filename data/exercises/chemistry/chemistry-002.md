## Problem

Balance the equation: $\text{H}_2 + \text{O}_2 \rightarrow \text{H}_2\text{O}$

## Solution

Count atoms on each side:

Unbalanced: H: 2 on left, 2 on right (✓); O: 2 on left, 1 on right (✗)

Add coefficient 2 to H₂O:

$$\text{H}_2 + \text{O}_2 \rightarrow 2\text{H}_2\text{O}$$

Now: H: 2 on left, 4 on right (✗); O: 2 on left, 2 on right (✓)

Add coefficient 2 to H₂:

$$2\text{H}_2 + \text{O}_2 \rightarrow 2\text{H}_2\text{O}$$

Balanced: H: 4 on each side; O: 2 on each side
