## Problem

Calculate the pH of a solution with $[H^+] = 1 \times 10^{-3}$ M

## Solution

pH is defined as: $\text{pH} = -\log_{10}[H^+]$

$$\text{pH} = -\log_{10}(1 \times 10^{-3})$$

$$\text{pH} = -(-3) = 3$$

The solution is acidic with a pH of 3.
