## Problem

A force of 100 N is applied to a mass of 25 kg. What is the acceleration?

## Solution

Using Newton's second law: $F = ma$

Rearranging: $a = \frac{F}{m}$

$$a = \frac{100}{25} = 4 \text{ m/s}^2$$
