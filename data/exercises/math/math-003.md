## Problem

Solve the system:

$$2x + y = 7$$

$$x - y = 2$$

## Solution

Using substitution method:

From equation 2: $x = y + 2$

Substitute into equation 1:

$$2(y + 2) + y = 7$$

$$2y + 4 + y = 7$$

$$3y = 3$$

$$y = 1$$

Then: $x = 1 + 2 = 3$

Solution: $(x, y) = (3, 1)$
