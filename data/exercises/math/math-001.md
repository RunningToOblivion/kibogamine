## Problem

Solve: $x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0$

## Solution

Using the quadratic formula: $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$

With $a=1$, $b=-5$, $c=6$:

$$x = \frac{5 \pm \sqrt{25 - 24}}{2} = \frac{5 \pm 1}{2}$$

Therefore: $x = 3$ or $x = 2$
